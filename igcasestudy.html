instagram case tuyd

Instagram Stories lets Instagram accounts post photos and videos that vanish after 24 hours. Stories were launched in August 2016, and now more than 500 million Instagrammers use stories daily.

Like adding effects to your photos, Instagram has story effects made by users of the app or Instagram itself for adding filters/effects onto your stories

The main problem I found after interviewing my friends was that it was difficult to locate the Instagram story effects with the current User Interface, this makes it hard for people to fully enjoy the feature

I wanted to find ways to enhance the User Experience when using Instagram stories. From using Instagram stories and from observing friends use it, I realised that there were a few personal favourite effects that each individual tends to use very often. Hence solution 3 which allows users to easily find their last used filter(s).
While trying to find a filter it is hard to see how the filter will look with the photo that the user wants to use hence proposing solution 2 which shows a variety of filters and is easy to visualise.
Lastly solution 1 was an easy way to allow users to put the filters into categories which they are able to name themselves so that they are able to find what filter they are looking for instead of typing the filter's unique name.
